# Narrative & Mechanics Design: The World-Machine

This document outlines the lore justifications and mechanical synergies requested to bring cohesion and depth to the idle looping mechanics. 

## 1. Lore Integration: Resolving the "Gamey" Mechanics

To make the mechanics feel grounded in the sci-fi setting, we recontextualize them away from "video game shops" and into "cybernetic survival mechanics."

### The "Step Store" -> Kinetic Cartography Link
**The Rationale:** You aren't buying things with a pedometer. Your character's exo-suit features a **Kinetic Mapping Dynamo**. The mothership/AI hovering in orbit is blind due to atmospheric interference. The only way it can accurately map the planet's topology and calibrate teleportation/manufacturing is by analyzing the micro-kinetic impacts of your footsteps. 
*   **The Transaction:** By walking, you generate localized topological data (Steps). You upload this data to the ship. In exchange for completing its map, the ship utilizes its orbital fabricators to beam down physical enhancements to your suit or alter the local terrain for you. 

### "Speed Tracks" -> Mag-Lev Conduits
**The Rationale:** Laying down "tracks" in a jungle is weird. Instead, you are deploying **Phase-Alloy Conduits**. 
*   **The Mechanic:** By expending stored kinetic energy/data, your suit micro-fabricates a strip of electromagnetic frictionless alloy onto the ground. 
*   **Why it makes you fast:** When your exo-suit boots step onto this conduit, they magnetically repel against it. You aren't just walking on a track; you are effectively turning yourself into a mag-lev train, gliding effortlessly across the terrain.

## 2. The Biome Devices: Interconnected "Clickers"

We want to move away from "just another drill" and create unique interactive devices in each biome. More importantly, these devices should feed into one another, creating a web of dependencies that makes interacting with them highly rewarding.

### A. The Mine: Deep Core Drill (The Foundation)
*   **Story:** An industrial percussive drill tapping into the planet's mantle to extract dense minerals and geothermal energy.
*   **Mechanic:** Traditional rapid-clicking/tapping to penetrate strata.
*   **Value:** Yields heavy raw materials (iron, carbon, gold). 
*   **Synergy Output:** Generates **Geothermal Heat Cells**. These cells are critical for operations in the Frozen Tundra.

### B. Verdant Maw: Bio-Resonance Siphon (The Accelerator)
*   **Story:** A delicate extraction device tapped into the hyper-accelerated root system of the alien jungle. It uses sound waves to safely extract organic enzymes without aggressive cutting.
*   **Mechanic:** A rhythm-matching mini-game. A ring expands and contracts, and the player clicks when it aligns with the target zone. Rhythm builds a multiplier.
*   **Value:** Yields organic materials (resin, fiber, biomass).
*   **Synergy Output:** Yields **Catalytic Enzymes**. These enzymes act as a biological lubricant for the Deep Core Drill, significantly increasing its base clicking power or passive idle speed.

### C. Lagoon Coast: Tidal Centrifuge (The Stabilizer)
*   **Story:** A massive spinning turbine anchored in the toxic/saline lagoon. It rapidly separates heavy water and atmospheric coolants from the sludge.
*   **Mechanic:** "Spin-to-win." The player clicks and drags their mouse/finger in a circular motion to build up RPM on the centrifuge.
*   **Value:** Yields silica, quartz, and clean water.
*   **Synergy Output:** Yields **Cryo-Coolant canisters**. The Deep Core Drill and advanced Factory machines generate massive heat. Coolant is required to push the Drill into the deepest, most lucrative strata (Stratum 5+) without anomalous meltdowns.

### D. Frozen Tundra: Cryo-Stasis Defroster (The Multiplier)
*   **Story:** The tundra is littered with ancient, crashed alien technology encased in unbreakable permafrost. The Defroster is an array of thermal lasers designed to carefully melt the ice.
*   **Mechanic:** "Charge and Release." The player holds the click to build up laser intensity, releasing just before the "burn limit" to flash-melt the ice. Going over the limit wastes the charge.
*   **Value:** Yields titanium, tungsten, and rare salvaged tech.
*   **Synergy input/Output:** The Defroster *requires* the **Geothermal Heat Cells** generated by the Drill to function. In return, it yields **Superconducting Coils**. These coils permanently reduce the PP (Processing Power) cost of everything in the Tech Tree and Factory by making the player's core processor vastly more efficient.

## The Synergy Loop Summary:
1. Play **Jungle Rhythm** to get Enzymes.
2. Use Enzymes to make the **Mine Drill** faster.
3. Spin the **Lagoon Centrifuge** to get Coolant.
4. Use Coolant to push the **Mine Drill** deeper to extract Heat Cells.
5. Take Heat Cells to the **Tundra Defroster** laser.
6. Play the Laser mini-game to get Superconductors.
7. Superconductors make the entire game (PP generation, upgrades, tech) drastically cheaper.
